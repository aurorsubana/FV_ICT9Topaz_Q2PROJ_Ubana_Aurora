Pancit Habhab

Ingredient Quantity
Miki Lucban 1 lb
Pork belly thinly sliced and chopped 1/2 lb
Beef broth 4 to 5 cups
Shrimp 1/2 lb
Pig's liver 1/4 lb
Chayote 1 piece
Pechay 1 cup
Snow pea 1 cup
Carrots 3/4 cup
Onion 1 medium
Garlic 5 cloves
Cooking Oil 4 tablespoons
Soy Sauce 5 tablespoons
Black Pepper 1/2 teaspoo

Instructions

  1. Prepare noodles, cook the pork and shrimp and prepare other necessities
  2. Put-in the onion and garlic. Cook for 2 minutes. Add the liver and stir.
  3. Add noodles and season it.
  4. Final touches and serve.
