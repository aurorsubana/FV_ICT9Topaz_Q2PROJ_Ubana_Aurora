Lucban Longganisa

Ingredient Quantity
Pork belly 1kg
Cane vinegar 1/2 cup
Pork fat 4 tbsp
Garlic 3 tbsp
Paprika 7 tsp
Salt 4 tsp
Sugar 2 tsp
Oregano 1 tsp
Dried hog casing or sausage casing 1 inch in diameter

Instructions

  1. Mix ground pork, fat, salt, smoked paprika, garlic, oregano, sugar, and vinegar in a mixing bowl. Allow flavors to develop and infuse for 30 minutes.
  2. Soak dried hog casing in warm water for 3 minutes, tie end, stuff meat mixture, and create links of longganisa 2-3 inches apart.
  3. Refrigerate for at least 8 to 12 hours before cooking or you can store it in the freezer.
